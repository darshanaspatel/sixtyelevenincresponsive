#sixtyeleven Tweets


sixtyeleven inc was set up by Sacha Judd, Milla Novak and Darshana Patel with the main goal to create a site that informed users of trending activities, things to do and places to go for people of all ages but mainly those that wanted further inspiration to keep them occupied in and around Wellington city. sixtyeleven inc came about through the inspiration found in an initial idea that looked at a travel site. This idea was further refined by using a specific location, Wellington (6011) and really pin pointing what made this location so special, from here the name sixtyeleven inc was born.
